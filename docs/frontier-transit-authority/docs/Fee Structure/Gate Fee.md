Gate fees are the fees associated with using a FTA-managed gate. They are configured and managed by the builder of the gate, or their delegated representative.

Gate fees are specific to a single gate, not a pair of linked gates. When a jump permit fee is calculated, the gate fees for both the source and destination gates are included. This per-gate fee structure allows for future development of a dynamically-linked, reconfigurable gate network.
