To protect the FTA from those who would attempt to harm it, punishments are necessary for those who cause harm. Punishment for damage to FTA infrastructure, or aggressive actions _near_ FTA infrastructure, takes two forms:
1. A [bounty](../Penalties/Bounties.md) on the head of the character who took the aggressive action
2. A [penalty](../Penalties/Blacklist.md) that increases the fees for that character to use the FTA network

Bounties are funded by a shared bounty pool that collects a percentage of all FTA revenue (see [fee structure](../Fee%20Structure/index.md)), specifically to be paid out for bounties. The bounty pool can only be paid out for kills of characters with bounties and cannot be accessed/withdrawn by the developers.
