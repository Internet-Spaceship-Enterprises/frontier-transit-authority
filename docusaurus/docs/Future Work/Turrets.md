In order to assist with the defence of the FTA network, FTA will allow builders to transfer ownership of turrets. The builders of the turrets will receive compensation for any bounty/defensive kills achieved by the turret.

Currently, this is not feasible as Killmails do not presently offer a way to see how multiple different turrets contributed to a kill. CCP is intending to add that functionality, at which point this system will be implemented.
